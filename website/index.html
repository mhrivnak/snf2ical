SnF Schedule

Usable calendars for the Sun 'n Fun Aerospace Expo

What

Use these links to import the Sun 'n Fun Aerospace Expo schedule into calendar software of your choice. They've been tested with Google Calendar.

Events are categorized into several files, so you can view just one at a time without so much clutter that it becomes difficult to use. Suggestions are welcome on the category choices.

Why

I have no connection to the expo. I'm just a first-time attendee in 2022 who wanted to look at the schedule, found the expo's online schedule to be unusable, and decided to convert their data into iCalendar files that could be imported into standard calendar software.

How

Copy each link, and use it to import a calendar into your calendar software. For example, Google Calendar lets you create a new calendar from a URL. Scroll down to the "Use a link..." section in their documentation.

No Guarantees

This data might be wrong. It's only as correct as what the expo published on their website. And while I don't think my conversion mangled the data, it might have.

If an event is important to you, verify the time and location through a primary source.

Updates

I have no idea if, or how often, the source data changes on the SnF website. But for good measure, these iCalendar files are re-generated every hour.

Note that software such as Google Calendar may not re-load the data from my links for several hours or longer.
